About the Project

Our Zero Waste & Zero Hunger Project supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 2: Zero Hunger and SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production). We aim to reduce food waste and redistribute surplus food to those in need.

Our Goals

Reduce Food Waste

Promote composting, recycling, and responsible consumption practices.

Feed the Hungry

Partner with local communities to distribute surplus food to vulnerable groups.

Raise Awareness

Educate people about sustainable food systems and environmental protection.

What We Do

  • Organize food donation drives
  • Conduct sustainability workshops
  • Support local farmers
  • Encourage zero-waste lifestyle practices
